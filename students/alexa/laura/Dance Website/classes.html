Classes Nearby

All the classes listed below have been tested and approved by dancers.






Dance School Infiniti:


Class Type: Ballroom and Modern
Description:
Infiniti Dance School is by far one of the best studios in the greater Seattle area. I have attended this studio for only 2 years, yet I truly feel as part of the family. The community there is amazing, all the girls get along and the coaches are like our friends. Each year Infiniti hosts a much anticipated end of the year performance that showcasses all the hard work put into the training. The head coach of Infiniti comes from the most famous dance crew in Russia; Todes.
Classes:
During the classes, time is put into warmup, stretching, technique, and choreography that is performed at the end of the year. Infiniti offers ballroom classes led by Umario and modern classes led by the head coach; Olga. Umario is a professional ballroom dancer with multiple studios in San Francisco and Washington. He also participated in So You Think You Can Dance as a choreographer. The classes are sorted by age and any dance background is accepted, even none! Each class is 1.5 hours and classes are 2 times a week. If you are looking for somewhere to start with a very supportive atmosphere, then Dance School infiniti is defintely for you!

Westlake Dance Center:


Class Type:
Hip-Hop, Jazz, Lyrical/Contemporary, Movement Theory, Ballet, Yoga, Stretch and Strength, Vogue, Progressions, Diamond Diva Beats, Waacking, Locking, and Urban Contemporary
Description:
Westlake has 2 studios that offer many dance styles. All the instructors are very skilled professionals and all the students create a positive atmosphere.
Classes:
Unlike Infiti, the classes have no strictb schedule, allowing walk-ins. The class schedule is posted on the westlake website. Dancers may attend the classes that they are interested in at the given time and studio location. The walk-in policy allows students to work around their schedule and are not bound to a certain time and day in the week.
