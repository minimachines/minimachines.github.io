Lion Dancing

Lion dancing is a form of entertainment at Chinese holidays, weddings or special events. It is connected to kung fu since only the kung fu students performed and practiced these dances. Lion dancing is actually very hard which is why only the kung fu students can do it for a long time.

Southern lion

The southern lion is a type of lion which is more commonly known. It consists of two people; a head and a tail.

In this picture you can see the head which is usually about 20-40 pounds(they make lighter ones now). They are always super colorful and the head person can flap the eyes and open the mouth.

Here you can see the head and the tail with the tail person bending over so the body waist up is parallel to the floor.

Northern Lion

The Northern Lion is the less commonly known lion and it is a two person lion with a head like a shaggy dog. There are also baby northern lions which are suits with a head but they are less common.
