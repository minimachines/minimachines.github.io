Hung Gar Kung Fu

Have you ever been interested in kung fu or in martial arts in general? In this website you will learn about a style of Kung Fu called Hung Gar which focuses on low, stable stances and being rooted to the ground. You will learn about different punches, kicks, blocks, stances and more.
