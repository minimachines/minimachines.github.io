Glossary

In this glossary you will learn all the different Hung Gar elements or moves.

Punches/Strikes

ping chooi - Front strait punch

chong chooi - Upper cut punch or hook punch

fu jau - tiger claw strike, usually aiming for the eye.

yaat chi chooi - half twisting or vertical punch.


Kicks

deng gek - Round house kick

tek gek - Front snap kick

yang gek - Side kick


Blocks

muk sau - Block with back of arm

long sau - High block with back of arm

so sau - circular block

chun sau - open hand block


Stances

sae-ping ma - The horse stance, feet planted on the floor, knees bent, facing the front.

goong hsi ma - The bow and arrow stance, facing your front knee, back leg strait, front leg bent and slightly inward.

tiu li ma - The cat stance, back leg very bent front leg slightly bent facing forward.



*These are not all of the Hung Gar moves, these are just some main ones.
