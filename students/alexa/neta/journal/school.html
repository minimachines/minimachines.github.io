From School To Choices





Now, when people think school they usually think two things:

Fun or Boring.

But I think...

CHOICES- oh no!




You see, I'm not really good when it comes to choices unless someone says something like

"Ok, if you can't decide then we are taking this one" or "We are taking this one in five, four, three, tw--"

"No, I want this one then" I would say or something like "Oh yeah, let's take that one"

Soon, the school year of 2017-2018 will begin, and guess what, I can't decide on my after school classes.
