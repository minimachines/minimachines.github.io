Something More Calming





When I get upset, try to keep away from elecronics, or just feel like

calming down, I go to my most trusted companion, or should I say companions-

Yes, that's right, books...

So far my latest favorite is called

The Siren

I usually only cry when I watch a super depressing movie like The Good Dinosaur



This was one of the first books, not movies, where I almost actually cried.

I don't know about you, but for me, this fact is really quite surprising.
